Recipe 1

Fry Salmon fish

Ingredients

  • 1 salmon steak
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 cajun seasoning
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 spring of dill

Instructions

  • Rub both sides of the salmon with the sea salt, pepper and cajun seasoning.
  • Melt the butter in large skillet over medium heat.
  • Add the salmon.
  • Fry the salmon for 3 minutes on the first side.
  • Turn over then cook the other side until cooked through about 3 -4 more minutes.
  • Put fish on the plate and garnish with dill for presentation.
